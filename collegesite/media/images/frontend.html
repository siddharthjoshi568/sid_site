Most importantly I will be giving some quizzes and exercises to test your skills on a regular basis. I will be providing questions and then you have to try solving them. This will enhance your programming skills and it will also help you become a good and a pro programmer.

Programming allows us to minimize manual work. With the help of programming, we can make scripts that can do our work at a much faster rate.
